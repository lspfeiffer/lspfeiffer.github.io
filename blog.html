Tower of London
January 6, 2014

Seeing the Tower of London was pretty amazign. I've always heard so much about it from stories and history classes but actually being there was a whole different experience. We got to see the crown jewels and learn more about the history of some of the British monarchs. Turns out that a lot of gifts were given to the kings and queens in the form of animals. It was funny to imagine how a Polar Bear would fare in the temporate British climate.
